Funding

Humanities Data Inquiry has been funded in whole or in part by three major grants from the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC), with additional support from the Digital Research Alliance of Canada and the University of Lethbridge.

  • CA$200,000 2017–2019

    Future Commons: Transforming Scholarly Communication through Collective Action

    SSHRC Partnership Development Grant · 890-2016-0081

    Preliminary work as part of a larger project on collective action and Open Science.

  • CA$200,000 2021–2023

    Good Things Come in Small Packages: A Grassroots Community of Practice for Open and FAIR Humanities Data Practices

    SSHRC Partnership Development Grant · 890-2020-0095

    Initial work.

  • CA$300,000 2025–2029

    Resistance to Data: Understanding Data Use, Data Management, and Data Infrastructure in the “Traditional” Humanities through Historical, Comparative, and Ethnographic Study

    SSHRC Insight Grant · 435-2025-0705

    Current.
