Outputs & Collaborations

HDI is conducted in partnership with the Humanities Innovation Lab at the University of Lethbridge and through collaborations with researchers, cultural institutions, infrastructure organizations, and community partners across Canada and the United States.

Project outputs include

  • Research publications and comparative case studies
  • Digital humanities and cultural heritage projects
  • Infrastructure, governance, and policy frameworks
  • Best-practice and data ethics resources
  • Workshops, presentations, and training materials
  • Open-access and community-oriented digital scholarship initiatives
